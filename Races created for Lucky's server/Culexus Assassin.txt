Null Entity

Pariah

Animus Speculum (Ability1)

Etherium Synskin (Ability2)
You blink into and out of 100% invisibility including your weapons every 1.75/1.5/1.25/1/.75 seconds. Activating this ability gives you 100% invisibility for 6/7/8/9/10 seconds. 20 second cooldown.

Psyk-out Grenade
