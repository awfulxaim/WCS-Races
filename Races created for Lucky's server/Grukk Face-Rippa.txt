Grukk Face-Rippa

R8 and knife only.

Orkish Growth
Your HP and max HP increases by 1/2/3/4/5 every 2 seconds. This resets when you spawn.

Stikkbomb (Ability1)
While your gun is out, toss an HE grenade. It has a 10/20/30/40/50% increased AoE and increased volume.
While your knife is out, your next hit with the knife causes an HE grenade explosion dealing damage and knockback to yourself and all enemies in the area (+ the bonus AoE).
15 second cooldown.

Bosspole
You and teammates in a 250 unit radius deal 10/20/30/40/50% extra damage.

Power Klaw
Your right click knife attacks slow enemies by 50% for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. Hitting an enemy already slowed by your Power Klaw bashes them and deals 100% extra damage.

Berserk Fury
20/25/30/35/40% chance when you lose HP to gain 50% increased movement speed and deal double damage for 10 seconds.

WAAAGH!
You and teammates in a 750 unit radius gain 75% damage reduction but become knife only for 4/5/6/7/8 seconds. 30 second cooldown.
