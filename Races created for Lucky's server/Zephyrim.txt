Zephyrim

Knife, P250, and HE grenade only.

Adepta Sororitas Power Armor
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50 more HP and 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Rosarius
You have 10/20/30/40/50% spell immunity.

Plasma Pistol
Your P250 charges while you are holding it, dealing 10/20/30/40/50% extra damage after charging for 1 second. firing or putting the gun away restarts the timer.

Krak Grenade
Your HE grenades deal 10/20/30/40/50% extra damage and remove all armor from enemies that are damage by it.

Laud Hailer (Ability1)
You heal teammates (not yourself) in a 600 unit radius of you for 5/10/15/20/25 HP. you and healed teammates gain 15% increased movement speed for the next 10 seconds.

Power Sword
You deal 100/125/150/175/200% extra damage with your knife. This damage cannot be evaded, blocked, or reduced.

Zephyrim Pattern Jump Pack (Ultimate)
Gain 10/20/30/40/50% increased movement speed and begin flying. You have no accuracy penalty while flying. 3 second cooldown.
