Dark Eldar Succubus

Fiveseven, Decoy grenade, and knife only.

Impossible Elegance
You have a 20/25/30/35/40% chance to evade and 15/20/25/30/35% increased movement speed.

Impossible Beauty
4/8/12/16/20% chance when you get hit to root the enemy for 1.5 seconds.

Wychsuit
You take 10/20/30/40/50% less damage from hits to the right leg and right arm.

Haywire Grenade
Your decoy grenade creates a 300 unit radius ward that lasts 10 seconds. Enemies who enter the ward are debuffed, removing all of their buffs, setting their skill's proc chances to 0%, and silencing them for 10 seconds. 

Darklight Blast Pistol
Your Fiveseven has 30/50/70/90/100 extra ammo in mag, but no reserve ammo.

Venom Whip
Your right click knife attacks bash for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. Your right and left click knife attacks poison the enemy for 5 damage per second for that duration.
