L4DI: Infected

Knife only. All Infected take 20 extra damage from grenades.

Mutated Endurance- Regenerate 10 HP/sec to a max of 100.
Mutated Speed- Gain with 145% speed when an enemy on the map is beaconed.
Dark Corners- Spawn with 85% invisibility
Distraction- Enemies who come within ward range of Infected are beaconed.
Panic- 25% chance when hit to respawn all Infected on your team to you.
Horde- Teammates in ward range are healed for 10 HP/sec to a max of 100.
