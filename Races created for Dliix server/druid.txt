Druid

USP-S and knife only. Can only be healed to a max of 175 unless in bear form.

Regrowth (ability)
Heals the ally you aim at (or yourself if no ally is aimed at) for 20, then 2/4/6/8/10/12/14/15 every 2 seconds for 12 seconds. 10 second CD

Rejuvenation (ability2)
Heals the ally you aim at (or yourself if no ally is aimed at) for 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 every 2 seconds for 12 seconds. 10 second CD

Moonfire
10% chance on hit to deal 5 extra damage, then burn that enemy for 5 damage every 2 seconds for 6/8/10/12/14/16/18/20 seconds.

Starfire
5% chance on hit to deal 10 extra damage, and 5 damage to all enemies in an increasingly larger area as this skill is leveled.

Swipe
When you deal damage with the knife deal 30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100% of that damage to nearby enemies. (Similar range to roots)

Mangle
50% chance when you deal damage with the knife to deal 1/2/3/5/7/9/12/15 extra damage.

Mark of the Wild
Your team (including you) spawns with helmet and vest armor, 1/2/3/5/7/9/12/15% increased movement speed, and 6/8/10/12/14/16/18/20 more HP.

Entangling Roots (ultimate)
Same as night elf.

Wild Growth
Heals 5 allies that are at the lowest HP (including yourself) for 5/10/15/25/30/35/45/60, then healing for half that, then half again, and so on, every second for 6 seconds. 18 second CD.

Lifebloom
Heals the ally you aim at (or yourself if no ally is aimed at) for 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 every second for 15 seconds, after 15 seconds heal them again for 40. 15 second CD.

Sunfire
10% chance on hit to deal 10 extra damage, then burn that enemy for 5 damage every second for 2/4/6/8/10/12/14/16 seconds.

Shooting Stars
20% chance when Sunfire procs or deals a tick of burn damage to deal that enemy an additional 1/2/3/5/7/9/12/15 damage.

Maim
10/15/20/25/35/45/60/75% chance on hit that each proc on an enemy is more powerful. The first proc stuns for 1 second, the next 2 seconds, increasing each time. this resets at the start of each round.

Thrash
When you deal damage with the knife you have a 50% chance to make the enemy and all nearby (200 range) enemies bleed for 1/1/1/1/2/3/4/5 damage per second for 15 seconds.

Shapshift
when you spawn you get a menu asking you to select a form. Your options are:
Tree (You take 20% less damage from guns and knives. Regrowth and Rejuvenation become Wild Growth and Lifebloom)
Moonkin (You deal 20% more damage with the USP-S. Moonfire and Starfire become Sunfire and Shooting Stars)
Cat (Knife only, you get 30% more movement speed and 50% invisibility. Swipe becomes Maim)
Bear (Knife only, you get 320HP and take 25% less damage from all sources. Mangle becomes Thrash)
