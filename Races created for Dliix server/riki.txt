Smoke Screen
you get a smoke every 20/17/17/14/14/11/11/8 seconds. Enemies in your smoke are "silenced" and have a 0/15/30/45/60/75/75/75% chance to deal no damage with hits. "Silenced" means they cannot proc skills or shopmenu items, and cannot activate abilities or ultimates.

Blink Strike (ability)
Teleport behind the player you are aiming at up to 950 range away. if it is an enemy they are slowed by 50% and take 2/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 damage. can be activated twice before the cooldown starts. 25/21/21/17/16/13/13/9 second cooldown.

Tricks of the Trade (ability2)
Become invulnerable and rooted for 2 seconds. All enemies in a 300 radius around you take 25 damage per second for the duration. 18/16/15/14/13/12/11/9 second cooldown. plays a sound while active.

Cloak and Dagger
on spawn and 9/8/7/6/5/4/3/2 seconds after attacking become 100% invisible and gain 10% more movement speed. when you attack (but not when using abilities) you lose all invisibility. you get 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40 extra XP per kill.
