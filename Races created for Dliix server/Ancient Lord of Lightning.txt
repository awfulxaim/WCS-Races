Ancient Lord of Lightning

Spawns with a Zeus, but is not restricted to any weapon.

Arcing Cascade
pressing jump while in the air forces you to the ground and releases 5 rings of lightning that expand outward from you when you land. these rings expand to a radius of 650 units horizontally with a height of 10 units and deals 3/5/9/12/15/18 damage to enemies who are hit by a ring. An enemy can only be affected by one ring per activation. it takes 5 seconds for the rings to reach their limit of expansion. 5 second cooldown.

Galvanic Arrow
Missed shots create a 250 unit radius ward that deals 25/30/35/40/45/50% of gun damage to enemies that enter it and lasts 2 seconds. 100% chance on hit to deal 4/8/12/16/20/25% extra damage to all enemies in a 250 unit radius of the enemy you hit.

Lightning Rod (ability)
Instantly teleport within 150 units of the nearest enemy that you have damaged this round and swap weapons to the Zeus. 10/9/8/7/6/5 second cooldown. The Zeus still has its natural recharge time.

Stormcall (ability2)
You deal 2/4/6/8/10/12 damage to a random enemy. 25/23/21/19/17/15 second cooldown.

Tempest Shield
You take 1/2/3/4/5/6 less damage for each hit you've taken until you completely block a hit, then this resets. When you completely block a hit deal damage equal to the total damage reduction you had to the enemy who hit you.

Charged Dash (ultimate)
While holding ultimate, set your player model to light blue, set your movement speed to 200/220/240/260/280/300%, become invulnerable, and lose all your weapons for up to 3/4/5/6/7/8 seconds. while this is active you deal damage equal to 5 times the number of seconds you have been dashing to enemies in a 200 unit radius of you every second. When the duration ends you do not get your weapons back for 2 seconds. 7/6/5/4/3/2 second cooldown.
