Timeshifter

cannot buy boots of speed or winged boots.

LX555 Checkpoint System (ability)
When activated you set your current location, when you activate this again you instantly appear back at that location. 50/45/40/35/30/25/20/15 second cooldown after second activation.

Time Loop Reincarnation Procedure
30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100% chance when you die to save the guns, grenades, armor, and kit you had when you died, and get them back when you spawn next. You have a 40/45/50/55/60/65/70/75% chance to respawn at your team's spawn point 6-12 seconds after you die chosen randomly. if you will respawn, you receive a message in chat letting you know as soon as you die.

Qx-5 Time Leech Catalyst
15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50% chance on hit to reduce the enemies movement speed by 5% and increase yours by 5% for 5 seconds. For each 5% movement speed you have greater than the enemy hit you deal 1 extra damage and heal for the amount of damage dealt.

Unravel Time
1/2/3/5/6/7/9/10% chance when hit to evade. When you evade you instantly appear where you were 2 seconds ago and double your movement speed for 2 seconds.

Chronos Timelock Suit
Spawn with 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% increased movement speed and 25/30/35/40/45/50/55/60% reduced gravity.

Phase Point Shift (ultimate)
For you and a random enemy a 5 second count down begins. after the count down ends, you and that enemy swap locations. If the enemy dies in the next 1/2/4/6/8/10/12/14 seconds you get exp as if you killed them. 15 second initial cooldown. 20 second cooldown.
