Archangel Overwatch Platform
Standing still decreases your damage taken. For every 2 seconds you stand still you take 1/1/1/1/2/2/3/4% less damage up to a maximum of 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% less damage taken. If you move more than 150 units away the damage reduction is lost.

Land mine (ultimate)
Roots you for 4 seconds then lays an invisible 50 unit radius ward. Stepping into the ward “arms” it. If that enemy leaves the ward area all enemies in a 150 unit radius take 250 damage. You can have up to 2 wards active at a time. Costs $500 to use. 15 second cooldown.
