Lady Geist

p250 and knife only.

Essence Bomb (ability)
Deal 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 damage to yourself and deal 10/15/20/25/30/35/40/45 damage to all enemies in a 210/220/230/240/245/250/255/260 unit radius of where you are aiming after 2 seconds. A green circle appears on the ground where the damage will be dealt. 10 second cooldown.

Life Drain (ability2)
The enemy you are aiming at takes 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 damage per second and you are healed for the same amount for up to 4 seconds. This ends if the enemy leaves a 900 unit radius of you or leaves line of sight. The enemy cannot activate abilities or ultimates while they are afflicted with Life Drain and your movement speed is reduced by .5 while this is healing you. 25 second cooldown.

Vitality
Spawn with 30/35/40/45/50/55/60/65 more HP.

Ghoulish Speed
Spawn with 1.06/1.10/1.14/1.18/1.22/1.26/1.30/1.36 movement speed.

Malice
25% chance on hit to lose 3/5/7/9/11/13/15/17 life but deal 1-1/1-1/1-2/1-2/1-3/1-4/1-5/1-6x that much damage rolled randomly. Enemies hit with this are slowed by .2 for 6 seconds and take 15% more damage from you for 16 seconds. this can stack up to 5 times. this cannot proc if you do not have the HP to lose.

Soul Exchange (ultimate)
You and the enemy with the most HP in a 210 unit radius swap HP. if you have more HP than that enemy this fails and the cooldown is refreshed. If you have less than 65/60/55/50/45/40/35/30 HP when you activate this, set that enemy's HP to 65/60/55/50/45/40/35/30 instead. Activating this refreshes the cooldown on all of your abilities. 160/150/140/130/120/110/100/90 second cooldown.
