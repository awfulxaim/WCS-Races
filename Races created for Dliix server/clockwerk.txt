Power Cogs (ability2)

Rocket Flare
you spawn with an HE grenade, you get another one every 20/18/18/16/16/14/14/12 seconds. enemies damaged by the grenade get a pulsing circle around them that makes a loud noise for 6/6/6/6/6/6/6/10 seconds.

Overclocking (ability3)
For 13 seconds, battery assault bashes all nearby (150 range) enemies instead of just one, you get a new HE grenade every 3 seconds, your hookshot bashes for 1.5 additional seconds, and you get 60% increased movement speed. after the duration is up you lose all the bonuses and move at 50% movement speed for 3 seconds. 50 second cooldown. you make a loud noise while this is active.

Hookshot (ultimate)
10/20/30/40/50/60/70/80% chance when you activate this ultimate you teleport instantly to the enemy you are aiming at. that enemy and all nearby (175 range) enemies are frozen for 1/1.2/1.4/1.6/1.8/2/2.2/2.4 seconds and take 4/8/12/16/18/22/26/28 damage. 60/60/60/60/45/45/45/30 second cooldown.
