Tasha the witch queen


Witch’s weaponry
Spawn with an M4A1-S and deagle 


Hideous laughter
2/4/6/8/10/12/14/15% chance on hit to prevent the enemy from moving or shooting for 3 seconds. They are still affected by gravity. They take 5 damage per second while they are laughing


Otherworldly guise
You are immune to extra damage and damage over time. You cannot be banished or rooted. Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% increased movement speed and 15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50% reduced gravity. You spawn with 100 armor and helmet.


Caustic Brew (ability)
Lay six 50 radius wards in a line in front of you that last 1 minute. Any enemy that steps in a ward takes 2-8 damage per second for 3/4/5/6/7/9/8/10 seconds. 45 second cooldown. Each damage tick is rolled randomly.


Mind whip
1/2/4/5/6/8/9/10% chance on hit to remove all ammo from the enemy’s magazine.


Demonomicon of Iggwilv (ultimate)
When you activate this, choose an effect to turn on.
Dolor: 1/1/1/2/2/3/4/5% chance on hit to move the enemy to your team for one round.
Ensnarement: When you die, respawn yourself and 1/1/1/1/2/2/2/3 teammates at spawn. Once per round.
Exaction: the first teammate that dies after you choose the effect is respawned near you. You and they share experience for kills this round.
Implore: 1/2/3/4/5/6/7/8% chance on hit to instantly teleport the enemy back to their spawn.
Imbrue: The next time you kill an enemy you gain 100 HP and you deal 5 extra damage on hit for the rest of the round. Procs once per round.
Minimus Containment: 1/1/1/2/2/3/4/5% chance on hit to freeze an enemy for 10 seconds but they take no damage for that duration.
Torment: 60/65/70/75/80/85/90/95/100% chance when hit to deal 100% mirror damage.
