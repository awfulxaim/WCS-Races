Coyote

Spawns with a P250.

Scavenge
When an enemy dies, you gain 1/2/3/4/5 HP, 200/250/300/350 money, 1/2/3/4/5 ammo in mag, and 3/6/9/12/15 ammo in reserve.

Cornered
When below 15/25/35/45/55 HP, add 10 extra damage to your hits.

Pack Tactics
When you hit an enemy that is within 350 units of one of your teammates you deal 60/70/80/90/100% extra damage.

Sneak Attack
When you have been out of combat for 18/16/14/12/10 seconds, your next shot deals 20-50 extra damage rolled randomly.

Hidden in Plain Sight
Spawn with a disguise and 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Last Lash
When you take lethal damage, deal 25/50/75/100% of the damage you took from that hit back to that enemy.
