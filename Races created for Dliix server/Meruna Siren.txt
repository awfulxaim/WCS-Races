Meruna Siren

Ebb and Flow (ability)
weaker human dash but backwards. for .5 seconds after activating this ability you have 100% evade chance. 13/12/11/10/9/8/7/6 second cooldown.

Tsunami
1/2/4/5/6/8/9/10% chance on hit to push the enemy and all enemies in 150 units of that enemy back 400 units and deal 6/8/10/12/14/16/18/20 extra damage to them.

Summon Storms (ability2)
for the next 12 seconds all enemies that enter a 600 unit radius around you take 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 damage every 2 seconds and are frozen for .5 seconds when they take that damage. 60/55/50/45/40/35/30/25 second cooldown.

Brine Orb
You get 1/1/2/2/3/4/5/6 grenades per round, given to you 3 seconds after the last grenade was thrown. enemies hit by the grenade are slowed by 100%. this slow decays over time, slowing by less each second until the enemy is back to normal speed. slow decreases by 10% every second.

Siren Screech (ultimate)
Deal 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/24 damage to all enemies in a 400 unit radius and banish them for 4 seconds. you and teammates in that unit range get 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% increased movement speed for 4 seconds. 55/50/45/40/35/30/25/20 second cooldown.
