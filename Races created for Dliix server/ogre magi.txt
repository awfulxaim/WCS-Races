Ogre Magi

knife and deagle only.

Fireblast
17% chance on hit to deal 7/13/19/25/31/37/43/49 damage on hit and bash that enemy for 1.2 seconds. when this procs you lose 300/350/400/425/475/500/525/575 money, if you don't have that much, this will not proc.

Ignite (ability)
Burns the nearest 2 enemies for 2/3/4/4/5/5/6/7 damage per second and slowing them by 20/20/20/23/23/23/26/29% for 1/2/3/4/5/6/7/8 seconds. 17 second cooldown. when you activate this you lose 375/400/425/450/475/500/525/550 money, if you don't have that much, this won't activate.

Bloodlust (ability2)
You get 11/15/19/20/21/22/24/26% increased movement speed for 30 seconds. 28/26/24/22/20/18/16/14 second cooldown. when you activate this you lose 175/200/225/250/275/300/325/350 money, if you don't have that much, this won't activate.

Unrefined Fireblast
21% chance on hit to deal 15 flat + 80/90/100/110/120/130/140/150% of your current HP as damage on hit and bash that enemy for 1.2 seconds. When this procs you lose 35% of your current money.

Fire Shield (ability3)
For the next 3 hits or 25 seconds whichever happens first, you take 15/25/35/45/55/65/75/85% less damage and deal 16 mirror damage. 15 second cooldown.

Dumb Luck
You spawn with 150/165/183/200/216/232/246/260 HP. Increases your chance for multicast to proc by 1/2/3/4/5/6/7/8%. You do not get money from killing players or winning/losing rounds. Instead your money is set to $2000 on first spawn. at the beginning of every round you spawn with $800 more than the previous round. while you are alive you get $160 every 3 seconds.

Multicast
you have a 40/45/50/55/60/65/70/75% chance:
when proccing fireblast to bash for 1.8 seconds and deal double fireblast damage instead.
when activating ignite to burn the nearest 4 enemies instead.
when activating blood lust to also give your nearest teammate 10/14/17/19/20/21/22/23% increased movement speed for the duration.
when proccing unrefined fireblast to bash for 1.8 seconds and deal double unrefined fireblast damage instead.
when activating fire shield to also give your nearest teammate a fire shield too.
you have a 0/0/0/0/0/10/20/30% chance:
when proccing fireblast to bash for 2.4 seconds and deal triple fireblast damage instead.
when activating ignite to burn the nearest 6 enemies instead.
when activating blood lust to also give your 2 nearest teammates 10/14/17/19/20/21/22/23% increased movement speed for the duration.
when proccing unrefined fireblast to bash for 2.4 seconds and deal triple unrefined fireblast damage instead.
when activating fire shield to also give your 2 nearest teammate a fire shield too.
you have a 0/0/0/0/0/0/0/15% chance:
when proccing fireblast to bash for 3 seconds and deal quadruple fireblast damage instead.
when activating ignite to burn the nearest 8 enemies instead.
when activating blood lust to also give your 3 nearest teammates 10/14/17/19/20/21/22/23% increased movement speed for the duration.
when proccing unrefined fireblast to bash for 3 seconds and deal quadruple unrefined fireblast damage instead.
when activating fire shield to also give your nearest 3 teammates a fire shield too.
