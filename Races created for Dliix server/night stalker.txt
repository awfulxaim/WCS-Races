Crippling Fear (ability)

Hunter in the Night
While it is dark you get 19/22/25/28/31/34/37/40% increased movement speed, 25% chance to block bash, freeze, sleep, slow, knockback, drug, shake, banish, or impale. Killing an enemy while it is dark heals you for 30 HP and gives you 25% more money.

Darkness (ultimate)
Darkens the screen of all enemy players by 25% for 40 seconds. When activated your crippling fear cooldown is refreshed. 40 second initial cooldown. 140/130/120/110/100/90/80/70 second cooldown.
