Sadism
Killing a sleeping enemy grants you 10/20/30/40/50 HP.

Dream Demon (ultimate)
When a teammate dies you have 3 seconds to press ultimate to instantly teleport to the location they died at. You are 100% invisible for 1 second and you gain 50 HP. If you are dead, once per round, you can activate this to respawn at that location with 50HP. 15 second cooldown.
