Water Spirit

Spawns with a glock. Not restricted to any weapon.

Water Up
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30% increased movement speed.

Water Beam (ability)
Your hits deal 5 extra damage on hit and push enemies away from you for 6 seconds. increased push with each level. 20/18/16/14/12/10 second cooldown.

Water Grenades
You HE grenades slow by 10/20/30/40/50/60% for 2/3/4/5/6/7 seconds.

Thirsty! (ability2)
You gain 1/2/3/4/5/6 charges every time you hit an enemy. Press ability2 to lose all your charges and heal for the amount of charges you lost.

Gone Teleport (ultimate)
Human teleport. If you hit a wall root yourself there and become 100% invis and glock only. While frozen you have standing accuracy. Your invisibility slowly decays while frozen. Landing on the ground resets all buffs/debuffs from this skill. 7/6/5/4/3/2 second cooldown.
