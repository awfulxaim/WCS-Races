Lens of Truth
You see the outline of enemies that are 70% or more invis and within 400/500/600/700/800/900 units of you.

Magic Arrows (ability)
You get a scout with 1 shot. 14/12/10/8/6 second cooldown. 50% chance on hit with the scout to burn the enemy for 2.5 seconds, blind the enemy for 1.5 seconds or paralyze the enemy for 1 second.

Longshot (ability2)
Pull yourself toward the location you are aiming. similar to spiderman. cooldown decreases with levels.

Golden Gauntlets
30/40/50/60/70% chance on kill to gain 5/10/15/20/25 HP.

Fairy Magic (ultimate)

Spin Attack

Din's Fire
Burn all enemies in a 400/450/500/550/600 unit radius for 3 seconds. 35 second cooldown.

Farore's Wind
Mark your current location and human teleport where you are aiming, activate ultimate again to instantly teleport back to your marked location. distance increases with levels, cooldown decreases with levels.

Nayru's Love
Become invulnerable for 2/2.5/3/3.5/4 seconds. You cannot activate your ultimate or abilities while invulnerable.
