Robin Hood

Scout race

Steal from the rich and give to the poor- Take the HP of the highest HP enemy and give it to you, that enemy's HP is then set to your previous HP. (ability)

Piercing his own arrow- The second hit dealt to any enemy increases damage dealt by 50%.

Roguish Trickery- 30% chance to evade, when you evade you become invisible for 1 second.

Spirit of the Forest- Windwalk, 25 second C/D (Ability2)

Inspiration- When you head shot an enemy you and all your teammates gain 20 HP.

Lie in waiting- When crouched become 90% invisible.
