Dunn

Protect- when you would take damage greater than 100 set Dunn's HP to 1
Blowback- ability, record last hit of damage Dunn has taken and add it to your next attack
Reinforce- headshot immunity, attacks to your head heal you for 100% of your current HP
Ricochet- ability2, block next incoming damage, add it to Dunn's next attack.
Payday- Dunn spawns with extra money equal to the HP he ended with last round.
Revitalize- Give 30 ammo and HP to Dunn instantly.
