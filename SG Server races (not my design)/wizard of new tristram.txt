Wizard of New Tristram

requires total level 4200. limited to 2 per team.

Firebird's Finery
If, ignoring damage reduction, evasion, and extra damage, the damage you have dealt to a player exceeds 150 damage: burn them for 1/2/3/4/5% of the damage you have dealt to them every .1 second for 2.5 seconds. each additional hit on that enemy updates the damage and refreshes the duration. this resets every round.

Zel's Stone of Vengeance
You deal 2/3/4/5/6% extra damage for every 150 units away from you the enemy is to a maximum of 36% extra damage.

Blur
Reduce the damage you take by 1/3/5/7/8 damage per hit.

Storm Armor
100% chance when hit to deal 4/8/12/16/20% mirror damage.

Teleport - Wormhole (ultimate)
Instantly teleport where you are aiming up to 500 units away. if you press this again within 3 seconds of the last teleport, you can do it again with no cooldown up to 2 additional times. 20/18/16/14/12 second cooldown.
