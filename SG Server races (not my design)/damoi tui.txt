Damoi Tui

requires total level 4250. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, or HP.

Explicit Mods
You take 2/3/4% less damage from all extra damage sources for every 10 levels you have in this race.

Implicit Mods
Spawn with 1/2/3% increased movement speed and 3/4/5% chance to ignore bash for every 10 levels you have in this race.

Cleave
100% chance on hit to deal 10/20/30/40/50% of the damage you dealt to another enemy within 200 units of the enemy you hit to a maximum of 30 damage.

Cyclone
left click knife attacks deal 6/12/18/24/30 damage to everyone in a 150 unit radius. if you hit an enemy directly with your knife they do not take this damage.

Vitality
You and teammates in a 300 unit radius regen .3/.6/.9/1.2/1.5 HP per second.

Warlord's Mark
Enemies in a 300 unit radius have a 20/25/30/35/40% chance to be bashed when hit.
