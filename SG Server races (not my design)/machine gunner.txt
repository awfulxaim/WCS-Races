Machine Gunner

negev and knife only. requires total level 1500. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, claws of attack, orb of frost, or HP.

Special Bullets (ability)
Press ability to cycle through the following:
You have a 100% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 10/20/30/40/50/60% for 1/2/3/4/5/6 seconds.
You have a 100% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 1/1.5/2/2.5/3/3.5 seconds (35 damage over 3.5 seconds)
You have a 100% chance on hit to increase your movment speed by 5/10/15/20/25% for 6 seconds.
You have a 20% chance on hit to deal 100/125/150/175/200/225% extra damage.

Heavy Lifting
You spawn with 10/20/30/40/50/60 more HP.

Living Bunker
You have 25/35/45/55/65/75% damage reduction against grenades, including extra damage from grenades.

Standoff (ultimate)
Root yourself, you have standing accuracy, you have 15/25/35/45/55/65% damage reduction. press ultimate again to cancel these effects.
