Ruthless Mercenary

M4A1-S, HE, and knife only. requires total level 3800. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy flaming gloves of warmth, cloak of shadows, ankh, or HP.

Weaponry
Spawn with an M4A1-S. 5/10/15/20% chance on hit to bash

Carpet Bomb
Spawn with 1/1/2/2 HE grenades. HE grenades you throw explode into 1/2/3/4 more grenades.

Body Armor
Spawn with 100 armor, no helmet, and 10/20/30/40 more HP.

Helmet
If you have at least 1 point in Body Armor, you get a helmet with the armor. 25/50/75/100% chance to block 50% of the damage you take from the first headshot you take each round.

Weapon Handling
25/50/75/100% chance to ignore disarm.

Camouflage
You have 45/50/55/60% invisibility while standing still.

Scavenge
100% chance on kill to add 4/7/10/13 ammo to your magazine.
