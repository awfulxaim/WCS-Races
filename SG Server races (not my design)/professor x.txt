Professor X

knife only. requires total level 1800. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, claws of attack, or scroll of respawning.

Wheelchair
While crouched you have 50/60/70/80/90/100% increased movement speed. (effectively a 33% movement speed increase while crouched)

Bullet Stopper
You take 10/20/30/40/50/60% less damage from hits. 10/20/30/40/50/60% chance when hit to get 1 "bullet"

Telekinesis (ability2)
10/20/30/40/50/60% chance on hit to throw the enemy up into the air. Press ability2 to toggle this skill on or off.

Possession
10/20/30/40/50/60% chance on death to respawn. This can proc as long as you have one other teammate alive.

Return to Sender (ability)
Spend 4/5/6/7/8/9/10 "bullets". You deal 4 damage to the enemy you are aiming at for each "bullet" you spent.

Freeze Time (ultimate)
Lay a 60/120/180/240/300/360 unit radius ward that paralyzes all teammates and enemies who enter it, except players playing Professor X or Faceless Void. You and other players inside that ward are immune to gun damage and extra damage but still take knife damage. That ward lasts 1/1/1/2/2/3 seconds. 20 second cooldown.
