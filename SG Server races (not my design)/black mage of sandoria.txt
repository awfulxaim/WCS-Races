Black Mage of San d'Oria

requires total level 4000. limited to 1 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, thorns, or HP.

Respawn Steal
7/14/21/28/35% chance when an enemy respawns to block the respawn and respawn a teammate.

Frost Barrier
Reduces all damage you take by 1/3/5/7/9 and deal 1/3/5/7/9% mirror damage. This applies to extra damage and damage from abilities/ultimates/wards too.

Mana Steal
100% chance on hit to gain 1/2/3/4/5 armor and remove 1/2/3/4/5 armor from the enemy.

Thaumaturge Boots
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Purify
Remove all movement debuffs from you and teammates in a 500 unit radius every 9/7/5/3/1 second.

Convert (ultimate)
Lose 20/17/15/12/10 armor and gain 15 HP. 5 second cooldown.
