Tracer

dualies and knife only. requires total level 9500. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, or XP.

Pulse Pistols
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50/60% increased movement speed. Your dualies have infinite reserve ammo.

Blink (ability)
Instantly teleport to a location 500 units away in the direction you are moving. has up to 3 charges. costs 1 charge to activate. recover a charge every 8/7/6/5/4/3 seconds.

Recall (ability2)
Teleport to the location you were at .3 seconds before every .1 seconds for 1 second. 19/17/16/15/14/13 second cooldown.

Pulse Bomb (ultimate)
underhand throw a grenade. that grenade sticks to any surface and explodes after being stuck for 1 second. that grenade has a 400 unit radius and deals 150/200/250/300/350/400% of normal damage. 275 second cooldown. every 1 damage you deal with any weapon reduces the cooldown by 1 second.
