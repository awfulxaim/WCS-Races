Ranger of the Woods

requires total level 1100. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, ring of armor, orb of venom, or HP.

Trackless Step
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50% invisibility.

Barkskin
Spawn with 100/105/110/115/120 armor. Your armor absorbs 10/20/25/30/40% of damage you take.

Craft Weapon
Spawn with an M4A1-S with 40/45/55/60/65 reserve ammo. If you lived through the last round it has 50/60/65/70/80 reserve ammo instead.

Poison
33% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 5 damage every 2 seconds for 2/4/6/8/10 seconds. (25 damage over 10 seconds at max.) 5 second cooldown.

Heal Wounds (ultimate)
Root yourself for 1 second, you heal for 5/10/15/20/25 HP to a max of 100 HP. 2 second cooldown.
