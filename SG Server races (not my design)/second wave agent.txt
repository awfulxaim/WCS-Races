Second Wave Agent

requires total level 5400. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy boots of speed, claws of attack, mask of death, or HP.

Division Training
You gain an effect depending on the weapon you are holding.
SMGs: 6/12/18/24/30/36% increased fire rate.
Rifles: 4/8/12/16/20/24% extra damage on headshots.
Shotguns: 3/6/9/12/15/18% chance on hit to impale the enemy.
Pistols: Deal 1/2/3/4/5/6 extra damage on hit.
LMGs: You have 4/8/12/16/20/24% increased movement speed.

Stamina
You spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30 more HP and 20/40/60/80/100/120 armor and helmet.

Precision
100% chance on headshot to beacon the enemy for 1.5/3/4.5/6/7.5/9 seconds. That enemy and all enemies in a 200 unit radius of that enemy can be seen through walls. Beaconed enemies take 15% more damage from gun damage and extra damage sources.

Seeker Mines (ability)
lays 1/2/3/4/5/6 100 unit radius wards that slowly move towards enemies. when they reach an enemy they drop a live grenade and disappear. 40 second cooldown. you can only use this 3 times per round.

Recovery Link
You and teammates in a 400 unit radius of you are healed for 10 HP every 10/9/8/7/6/5 seconds. When you would take lethal damage this heals all teammates in that radius.

Survivor Link
You and teammates in a 400 unit radius of you gain 5/10/15/20/25/30% increased movement speed and take 2/4/6/8/10/12% less damage. When you or a teammate within a 400 unit radius of you would take lethal damage, they evade that damage and are healed for 10/20/30/40/50/60% of the damage they would have taken. this can proc on each player once every 30 seconds.

Tactical Link
You and teammates in a 400 unit radius of you deal 1/2/3/4/5/6 extra damage on hit and have 6/12/18/24/30/36% increased fire rate. this is only active while there is a teammate in that radius.
