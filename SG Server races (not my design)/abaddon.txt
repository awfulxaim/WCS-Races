Abaddon, Lord of Avernus

requires total level 2600. limited to 2 per team.

Mist Coil (ability)
Lose 10/9/8/6/5 HP. deal 15/18/21/23/25 damage to the enemy you are aiming at or heal a teammate you are aiming at for 30/33/35/38/40 HP. 20 second cooldown.

Aphotic Shield
Every 14/13/12/10/8 seconds, the next hit you take is reduced by 90%. When you take that hit deal 20/25/30/35/40 damage to all enemies in a 300 unit radius.

Curse of Avernus
40% chance on hit to slow and reduce the enemy's damage dealt by 25% for 1/1.5/2/2.5/3 seconds.

Borrowed Time (ultimate)
For the next 3 seconds, you cannot deal damage but you heal for 20/40/60/80/100% of the damage you would take instead. 45 second cooldown. your player model turns bright blue and has blue fire sfx on it while this is active.
