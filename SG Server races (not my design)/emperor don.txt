Emperor Don

requires total level 6250. limited to 2 per team.

Security
When an enemy enters a 300 unit radius of you they are rooted for 1 second and take 5/10/15/20 damage. 20 second cooldown.

Unstoppable
25/50/75/100% chance when you would be bashed to ignore it and bash that enemy instead.

Royal Robes
10/15/20/25% chance to evade damage. this includes extra damage.

Barricade (ultimate)
Create a wall. That wall has 100/200/300/400 HP. your teammates deal 4x damage to your walls. 30 second cooldown.
