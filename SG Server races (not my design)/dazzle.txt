Dazzle

pistols only. requires total level 5250. limited to 1 per team. cannot buy orb of venom.

Poison Touch
8/16/24/32/40% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 3 damage per second for 2 seconds. while they are poisoned they are slowed by 30%. after the poison ends they are bashed for 1 second.

Shallow Grave (ability)
the teammate you are aiming at cannot die for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. if they would take lethal damage their HP is set to 1 instead.

Shadow Wave (ability2)
Heal yourself for 2/4/6/8/10 HP. a teammate within 250 units of you is healed for 10 HP. this can jump from player to player, both teammates and enemies, gaining 2 damage/healing with each jump. When this jumps to an enemy they take damage rather than heal. 14 second cooldown.

Weave (ultimate)
All teammates and enemies within an 800 unit radius of the location you are aiming at gain .5/1/1.5/2/2.5% reduced/increased damage taken/given every second for 16 seconds. 28 second cooldown.
