Teron'gor

Has 100 max HP. requires total level 3000. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, or HP.

Soul Harvest
You regenerate .75/1/1.25/1.5/1.75/2 HP per second. 100% chance on kill to gain 1 soul and increase your HP regeneration by 1 HP per second for 8/10/12/14/16/18 seconds. this stacks and refreshes when you get another kill during that duration. souls last for the rest of the round.

Curse of Exhaustion
60% chance on hit to slow enemies by 40% for 1.5/2/2.5/3/3.5/4 seconds. In Death Knight form, this also lowers their damage dealt by 10% per soul you have for that duration.

Seed of Malevolence
100% chance on kill, that enemy explodes dealing 25/30/35/40/45/50 damage to enemies in a 200 unit radius. in Death Knight form, this deals 10 more damage for each soul you have.

Doom
30/40/50/60/70/80% chance on hit to poison the enemy dealing 4 damage per second for 4 seconds. this does not stack.

Demonic Leap
Spawn with 6/12/18/24/30/36% increased movement speed. In Death Knight form, you have a single long jump with a cooldown of 10 seconds, reduced by 1 second for each soul you have.

Death Knight (ultimate) (1 level)
paralyze yourself for 3 seconds. for that duration you take 90% reduced damage. After that duration set your HP to 100. Your skills bonus effects are now active for the rest of the round. 45 second initial cooldown.
