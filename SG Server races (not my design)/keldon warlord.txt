Keldon Warlord

SG556 and knife only. requires total level 3200. limited to 2 per team.

First Strike
10/20/30/40% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 10/20/30/40% for 1/2/3/4 seconds.

Double Strike
100% chance on hit if the enemy is slowed by First Strike to deal 20/30/40/50% more damage.

Whispersilk Cloak
You spawn with 125 armor and helmet and have a 25/50/75/100% chance to spawn with ultimate immunity.

Plunder
5/10/15/20% chance on hit to steal 250/500/750/1000 money from your enemy.

Advanced Weaponry
Your SG556 deals extra damage equal to 20/30/40/50% of the number of teammates you have.

Quieting Aura (1 level)
Enemies within a 600 unit radius of you are silenced. (aka they cannot activate abilities or ultimates)
