Hunter of Demons
requires total level 750. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy flaming gloves of warmth, long jump, boots of speed, or cloak of shadows.

Vault
Long jump. you spawn with 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Grenade
You spawn with an HE grenade. your HE grenades burn enemies damaged by it for 3 seconds (30 total damage)

Mark for Death
.2/.4/.6/.8/1% chance per damage dealt to a player to block them from respawning.

Smoke Screen
Spawn with 12/24/36/48/60% invisibility.

Awareness
When you would be hit for lethal damage block 50 damage of it and heal for 40 HP. 35/32/29/26/25 second cooldown.

Preparation (ultimate)
add 4/8/12/16/20 bullets to your magazine. 20 second cooldown.
