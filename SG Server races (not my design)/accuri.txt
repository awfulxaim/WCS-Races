Accuri, Master Marksman

Awp and knife only. Requires total level 2900. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy mask of death or HP.

Deadeye
You deal 10/11/12/13/15/17/19/20% more damage. this is not considered extra damage for the purposes of blocking extra damage and calculating weapon damage. (that means that leech considers this damage when it leeches) Enemies who survive your hit take extra damage equal to 10/15/20/25/30/35/40% of their remaining HP.

Ghillie Suit
Spawn with 25 more HP and 30/35/40/45/50/55/60% invisibility.

Precision
100% chance on kill to gain 2/4/6/8/10/12/14% increased movement speed and .5/1/1.5/2/2.5/3/3.5 HP regen every 2 seconds. this stacks. If you survive the round, you keep up to 2 kills worth of these bonuses.

Freeze Pistol
100% chance on hit to slow enemies by 15/25/35/45/55/65/75% for .5/1/1.5/2/2.5/3/3.5 seconds.

Teleport
you instantly teleport to the location of your nearest bullet impact. you can only activate this within 5 seconds of that impact. 20/18/17/16/14/12/10 second cooldown.
