T-Rekts

deagle and knife only.

requires total level 2500. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy claws of attack, ammo resupply, or HP.

Hunt (ability)
Activates the Apex Predator and Stomp Skill. 34/33/32/31/30 second cooldown.

Apex Predator
When you Hunt you gain 50% increased movement speed for 1/1.5/2/2.5/3 seconds.

Stomp
When you Hunt you deal 3/6/9/12/15 damage per second to enemies within 100 units of you. this lasts 5 seconds.

Powerful Jaws
You deal 4/7/10/13/16% extra damage on hit.

Thick Skin
You take 8/16/24/32/40% less damage from all sources.

Rekt (ultimate)
Your next shot deals 33/66/100/133/166% extra damage if you hit. 30 second cooldown.
