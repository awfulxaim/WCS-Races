Granthad, The Divine Knight

requires total level 500. limited to 3 per team. cannot buy mask of death, cloak of shadows, or HP.

Divine Speed
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30% increased movement speed.

Purifying Light
When you blind an enemy or an enemy tries to blind you, you heal for 2/4/6/8/10/12 HP.

Adjusted Eyes
Blinds and Flash effects on you are reduced by 50/60/70/80/90/100%.

Divine Ultimatum
You heal for 1/2/3/4/5/6 HP every 2 seconds.

Divine Deagle
You spawn with a Deagle with 5/10/15/20/25/30 more ammo in mag. Your deagle 50% blinds for .25/.5/.75/1/1.25/1.5 seconds on hit.

Flash (ultimate)
Drop a flashbang at your feet and become 100% invisible for 2 seconds. 25/23/21/19/17/15 second cooldown.
