White Mage of San d'Oria

requires total level 4000. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump, boots of speed, sock of feather, or HP.

Stone Skin
4/8/12/16/20% damage reduction from all sources. this double dips on extra damage.

Banish
Convert 5/10/15/20/25% of the damage you deal on hit to extra damage. You blind enemies hit by your extra damage for .5/1/1.5/2/2.5 seconds.

Haste
Spawn with 7/14/21/28/35% increased movement speed and 15/30/45/60/75% reduced gravity

Gift of the Magi
Spawn with 20/40/60/80/100 more HP.

Benediction (ultimate)
Respawn the last teammate who died. 160/150/140/130/120 second cooldown.
