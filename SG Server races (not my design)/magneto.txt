Magneto

knife only. requires total level 2200. limited 2 to per team. cannot buy long jump or ammo resupply.

Steel-Toed Boots
You spawn with 15/20/25/30/35% reduced gravity

Steel-Toed Boots II
You spawn with 10/20/30/40/50% increased movement speed.

Magneto's Helmet
20/40/60/80/100% chance to respawn with ultimate immunity.

Bullet Time
15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50/55/60% chance to evade.

Bullet Grab (ability)
you store 75% of the damage you take, when you press ability the next time you would evade or take a hit you deal that damage to the enemy who shot you. You can store up to 30/40/50/60/70 damage.

Metal Sticks (ultimate)
Slow all enemies in an 800 unit radius around you by 50% for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. 25 second cooldown.
