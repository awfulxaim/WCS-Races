Exile of Wraeclast

AK and knife only. requires total level 4750. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy HP.

Assassination
4/8/12/16/20% chance on hit with the AK to deal 60% extra damage.

Warrior's Blood
You gain .5/.75/1/1.25/1.5 HP per second.

Drop Chance
15/30/45/60/75% chance on kill to get a random shopmenu item.

Flask (ability)
Opens a menu allowing you to pick a flask to use. Flasks last 5/8/11/14/17 seconds and each can be used once per round. you can have up to 1/2/3/4/5 active at a time. Leveling this skill levels all the flasks, they are not separate skills.

Life Flask
Heal for 2/3/4/5/6 HP per second.

Mana Flask
Gain 5/6/7/8/9 armor per second.

Hybrid Flask
Gain 1/1.5/2/2.5/3 HP and armor per second.

Quicksilver Flask
Gain 8/16/24/32/40% increased movement speed.

Bismuth Flask
Become immune to burn, movement reducing effects, and extra damage.

Amethyst Flask
Become immune to poison.

Ruby Flask
Become immune to burn.

Sapphire Flask
Become immune to movement reducing effects.

Topaz Flask
Become immune to extra damage.

Aquamarine Flask
Reduce movement reducing effect durations on you by 20/30/40/50/60%. lay a 200 unit radius ward that lasts as long as this flask does, it slows enemies who enter its radius by 60%.

Basalt Flask
You take 5/10/15/20/25% less damage from hits.

Corundum Flask
You cannot be bashed.

Diamond Flask
Double the chance to proc Assassination.

Gold Flask
When Drop Chance procs you are more likely to get a more expensive item. 30% chance to get an item that is 5k or more, 10% chance to get an item that is 10k or more.

Granite Flask
You take 4/6/8/10/12 less damage from hits.

Iron Flask
You take damage from armor before HP.

Jade Flask
5/10/15/20/25% chance to evade.

Quartz Flask
You are not blocked by players. 2/4/6/8/10% chance to evade extra damage.

Silver Flask
100% chance on kill to deal 2 extra damage on hit for the duration of this flask. this stacks up to 4/5/6/7/8 times.

Stibnite Flas
4/8/12/16/20% chance to evade. when you evade you create a smoke cloud.

Sulphur Flask
You deal 8/16/23/32/40% more damage. lay a 200 unit radius ward that lasts as long as this flask effect, you and teammates who stand in this ward gain your current level of Warrior's Blood regen. this is not considered extra damage for the purposes of blocking extra damage and calculating weapon damage. (that means that leech considers this damage when it leeches)

Lightning Warp (ultimate)
After a 1.25 second delay, instantly teleport to where you are aiming. enemies in a 200 unit radius of where you teleported from and where you land take 5/10/15/20/25 damage. 15/12/9/7/5 second cooldown.
