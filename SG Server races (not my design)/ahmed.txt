Ahmed

requires total level 3100. limited to 3 per team.

Galil
Spawn with a Galil with 40/45/50/55/60 ammo per mag.

Glock
Spawn with a Glock with 25/30/33/40/48 ammo per magazine.

Adrenaline
100% chance when hit to increase your movement speed by 5/10/15/20/25% and reduce the damage you take from hits by that same amount for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. this does not stack.

Fires of War
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 1/1.5/2/2.5/3 seconds. (30 damage over 3 seconds)

Self Sacrifice (ultimate)
Kill yourself, you deal 25/50/75/100/125 damage to all enemies in a 300 unit radius. If you are killed before you activate this, you deal half that damage instead.
