Pyromaniac

HE grenade, incendiary, and knife only. requires total level 2000. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy flaming gloves of warmth, boots of speed, orb of venom, or ammo resupply.

Bombs Away
You get 1 HE grenade every 14/13/12/11/10 seconds and 1 incendiary every 35/30/25/20/15 seconds.

Proximity Mines (ability)
lay a 100 unit radius ward that is only visible to your team and lasts the entire round. this ward deals 5/10/15/20/25 damage and is destroyed when an enemy steps into its area. You can lay up to 1/2/3/4/5 mines per round. 2/1.5/1/.75/.5 second cooldown.

Fire Fire Everywhere
40/50/60/70/80% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 1 second. (10 damage over 1 second)

Bounce-Bounce-Boom
Your HE grenades take 1-7/1.25-5.5/1.5-4/1.75-2.5/1.5-1.5 seconds to explode rolled randomly.

Vest of Flames
Spawn wih 25/50/75/100/125 armor and helmet and 20/25/30/35/40% increased movement speed. You are immune to burn effects.

Blast Shield
You take 25/30/35/40/45% less damage from hits and 15/20/25/30/35% less damage from extra damage.

Explosive Dagger
15/30/45/60/75% chance on hit with the knife to knock the enemy 200 units away.

Special Delivery (ultimate)
get a flashbang. that flashbang deals 100 damage on hit and homes in on the nearest enemy to it. that flashbang does not blind when it pops. 70/60/50/40/30 second cooldown.
