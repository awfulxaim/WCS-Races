Ogre Stonethrower

smoke, flash, and knife only. requires total level 3500. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump or HP.

Power Throw
Direct hits with grenades deal 200/300/400/500/600 damage.

Unstoppable Force
Your grenade damage ignores evasion and damage reduction.

Flash Rock
When you throw your flashbang you get a new one 1/2/3/4/5 times per round.

Powder Stone
When you throw your smoke grenade you get a new one 1/2/3/4/5 times per round.

Muscle
Spawn with 20/40/60/80/100 more HP and 10/20/30/40/50% damage reduction from all sources. this double dips on extra damage.

Restock (ultimate)
Refill your grenades. Add 1/2/3/4/5 smokes and flashes to the number you can receive this round. 30 second cooldown.
