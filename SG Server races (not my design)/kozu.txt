Kozu The Stalwart

requires total level 1500. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy claws of attack, or HP.

Sturdy
Spawn with 20/25/30/35/40 more HP.

Strong
100% chance on hit to deal 2/3/4/5/6 extra damage.

Resistant
You take 1/3/5/7/8 less damage from hits and 20/25/30/35/40% less damage from extra damage.

Steadfast
You reduce the duration of bashes against you to .55/.45/.35/.25/.15 seconds.

Regeneration (ultimate)
Heal yourself for 5 HP per second for 4/5/6/7/8 seconds. Double the HP healed per second while you are standing still. 25 second cooldown.
