Super Soldier

requires total level 600. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy HP.

Sprint
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Veteran's Endurance
Spawn with 8/16/24/32/40 more HP.

Arm Up
20/40/60/80/100% chance to spawn with an M4A1-S.

Marksmanship
4/8/10/12/14% chance on hit to deal 250% extra damage.

Flaming Valor (ultimate)
60 second initial cooldown. Gain a 100% chance on hit to burn and slow the enemy by 25% for 2.5 seconds. this does not stack (25 damage over 2.5 seconds.)
