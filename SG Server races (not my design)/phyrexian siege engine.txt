Phyrexian Siege Engine

Scout, grenades, and knife only. requires total level 2750. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy long jump or HP.

Bioplasma Grenades (1 level)
Your grenades stick to the first surface they hit. You spawn with one of each grenade.

Blinding Abyss
Enemies blinded by your flashbangs are slowed by 10/20/30/40/50% for the duration of the blind.

Built for War
You deal 10/20/30/40/50% extra damage on hit.

Yawgmoth's Fire
You deal 1/2/3/4/5 extra damage on tick with your molotovs.

Flesh to Fuel
10/20/30/40/50% chance on kill to get one of each grenade, full ammo, and an additional ultimate use.

Impale Cannon (ultimate)
You get an AWP with 1 shot. enemies within 200 units of the location the shot lands are knocked backwards and rooted for 3 seconds. once per round.
