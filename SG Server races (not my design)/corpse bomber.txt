Corpse Bomber

requires total level 2600. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy mole, long jump, boots of speed, claws of attack, or HP.

Corpse Erasers
Your primary weapon deals no damage, instead that enemy gains an explosive charge on hit. Explosive charges deal 10/20/30/40 damage when they are detonated.

Corpse Speed
Spawn with 15/20/25/30% increased movement speed.

Corpse Evasion
6/9/12/16% chance to evade

Corpse Health
spawn with 15/25/30/40 more HP.

Detonation (ultimate)
detonate your explosive charges. Enemies in a 150/200/250/300 unit radius of an enemy with an explosive charge also take the damage that enemy took. 1 second cooldown.
