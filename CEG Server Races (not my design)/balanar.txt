Balanar

Day becomes night and night becomes day every 12 seconds. Skills can only proc or be activated at night and passive effects are turned off during the day. requires total level 740.

Hunter in the Night
Spawn with 8/16/24/32/40/48% increased movement speed.

Crippling Fear
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to paralyze the enemy for 1 second.

Traumatic Sight
100% chance on kill to slow all enemies in a 300 unit radius of the enemy you kill by 60% for 5 seconds.

Presence in the Night
There is a white line only you can see drawn from you to the nearest enemy. the max distance you can detect an enemy from is 500/600/700/800/900/1000 units.

Overwhelming Brutality
You deal 20/30/40/50/60/70% more damage to enemies with 50 or less HP and gain 5/10/15/20/25/30 HP on kill.
