Aelous

requires total level 300.

Spiraling Gale
3/5/9/12/15/18% chance on hit to make the enemy look directly away from you.

Gust of Wind
Your hit shots knock the enemy back, missed shots knock enemies away from the bullet impacts. the knockback increases with levels. grenades also knock enemies away from their center if enemies take damage from them.

Vortex Grenades
Your grenades pull enemies in a 150/200/250/300/350/400 radius towards the grenade before they explode.

Into Thin Air
20/30/40/50/60/70% chance to instantly teleport 50 units to left or right of where you were when you get shot. this can proc when you evade.

Distorting Wind
While stationary you deal 5/10/15/20/25/30% extra damage. you have a 4/8/12/16/20/24% chance to evade while moving.

Wings of Icarus (ultimate)
press ultimate to begin flying, while flying enemies in a 150/200/250/300/350/400 unit radius of you get pushed away from you once per second. 20/18/16/14/12/10 second cooldown.
