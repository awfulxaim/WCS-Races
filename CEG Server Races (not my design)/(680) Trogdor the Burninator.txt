Trogdor The Burninator

requires total level 680

Tiny Wings
spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30% increased movement speed.

Burninator Breath
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 2.5 seconds.

Burnination Wards (ability)
Lay a 200 unit radius ward that burns enemies who enter it for 3 seconds. lasts 15 seconds. 30 second cooldown.

Failure to Flight
Spawn with 35/40/45/50/55/60% reduced gravity.

Burninating the Countryside (ultimate)
Burn a random enemy for 2.5 seconds. 40/35/30/25/20/15 second cooldown.
