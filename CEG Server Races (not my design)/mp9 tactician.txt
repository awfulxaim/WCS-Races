MP9 Tactician

Spawns with and is restricted to the MP9. requires total level 300.

MP9 Rush!
You spawn with 9/28/27/36% increased movement speed.

Lift Off!
6/12/18/24/30% chance to set the enemy's gravity to 40/30/20/10% for 10 seconds. This cannot proc on an enemy already affected by it.

Supplies
Your MP9 has 5/10/15/20 more rounds in its mag and has double the reserve ammo.

Cybernetic Implant
5/10/15/20% chance on hit to gain 20/30/40/50% of the damage dealt back as HP.

Cyber CPR
50/60/70/80% chance to respawn where you died, once per round.
