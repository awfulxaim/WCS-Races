Barrel Boy

Spawns with a red rusty barrel as its player model for T or a blue rusty barrel for CT. Five-seveN only, no knife. requirs total level 1500

Pressure
30/40/50/60/70/80% chance when you die to deal 10/20/30/40/50/60 damage to all enemies in a 200 unit radius.

Copy Cat (ability)
Spawn a barrel where you are aiming that is the same color as you. 21/18/15/12/9/6 second cooldown.

Ricochet
7/14/21/28/35/42% chance to evade. when you evade nothing can proc and no message is shown in chat for the enemy.

Overfill
10/20/30/40/50/60% chance on kill to heal for 25/30/35/40/45/50 HP.

Swap Out (ultimate)
instantly teleport to the location of a barrel you are aiming at, destroying that barrel and creating a new one where you teleported from. 30/26/22/18/14/10 second cooldown.
