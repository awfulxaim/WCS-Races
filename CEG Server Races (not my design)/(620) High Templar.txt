High Templar

requires total level 620

Khaydarin Amulet
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30 more HP

Plasma Shield
Reduce the damage you take by 5/10/15/20/25/30%.

Psi Storm (ability)
Lay a 150 unit radius ward that deals 10 damage per second to enemies who enter its radius. this ward lasts 10 seconds. 20 second cooldown.

Phase Shift (ability2)
Become 100% invisible and invulnerable for 5 seconds. you cannot shoot while this is active. 20 second cooldown.

Protoss Armor (ultimate)
For the next 1/2/3/4/5 seconds when you would take lethal damage to evade that damage and instantly teleport back to spawn. 25 second cooldown.
