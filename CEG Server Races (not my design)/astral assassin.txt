Astral Assassin

Spawns with and is restricted to p90, deagle, and knife. requires total level 620.

Astral Armor
spawn with 25/50/75/100 armor, but no helmet and 8/16/24/32% increased movement speed.

Stop Time
4/6/8/10% chance on hit to paralyze the enemy for 1 second.

Hex
5/10/15/20% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 30% for 3 seconds.

Healing Aura
Every 5 seconds heal all teammates and yourself for 1/3/5/7 HP.

Boost Aura
Spawn with 5/10/15/20 more HP and 16/32/48/64% reduced gravity.

Phoenix
5/10/15/20% chance on kill to revive the first teammate who died and is still dead.
