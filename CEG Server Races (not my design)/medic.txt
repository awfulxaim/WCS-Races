Medic

requires total level 1010.

Syringe Gun
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 2 damage per second for 3 seconds and add two bullets to your mag.

Medigun
Shooting teammates heals them for 2/4/6/8/10/12 HP per shot.

Support
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30 more HP and 5/10/15/20/25/30% increased movement speed.

Blutsauger
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to leech 50% of the damage dealt.

Kritzkrieg (ultimate)
After healing or dealing 300 damage, pressing ultimate while aiming at a teammate within 300 units makes them deal 100% extra damage on hit for 8 seconds or until you leave a 300 unit radius of each other. Even if you deal or heal 200 damage you cannot activate this again until the 60/55/50/45/40/35 second cooldown ends.
