Only indication enemies receive shows in the chatbar that you have been tagged by "The Nightmare". On the first hit, that enemy becomes paralyzed for 0.1/0.3/0.45/0.6/0.75/.9 seconds. Cooldown 50/46/42/38/34/30 seconds.(Note: Other skills do not proc with ultimate) If there are more than one per team, paralyze is reduced evenly like Grim Reaper skill Mourning (2 Sleep paralysis would cause a max of .45 second paralyze, 3 would cause a max of .3 second paralyze at max levels)
