Osama Bin Laden

Spawns with and is restricted to AK, HE grenade, and knife. requires total level 680.

Death to Infidels!
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to gain 5 ammo.

Allahu Akbar!
10/20/30/40/50% chance when you throw a grenade to get a new one. 100% chance when you kill an enemy to get an HE grenade.

Hide in Shadows
spawn with 30/40/50/60/70% increased invisibility.

Anthrax Bullets
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to shake the enemy and impale them.

Terrorist Radar
A red line only you can see is drawn from you to enemies in a 200/250/300/350/400 unit radius every .25 seconds.
