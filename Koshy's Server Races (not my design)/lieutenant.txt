Lieutenant

Five-seveN and knife only

Hollow Point
10/20/30/40/50% chance on hit to deal 5-20 extra damage, rolled randomly.

Flare Shot
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to blind the enemy for 1 second.

Explosive Bullets
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to deal 50% of the damage you dealt to the enemy to other enemies in a 200 unit radius.

Bionic Vest
Spawn with 20/40/60/80/100 armor and helmet. While you have armor you have a 5/10/15/20/25% chance to evade.

Expert Marksman (ultimate)
Press ultimate to zoom in. zoom gets more usable with levels.
