Meet The: Medic

Spawns with MP5 and Knife.


Kritz- 20% chance on hit to deal 3x more damage.

Medigun- Like Lifebloom, Heals 25 HP per second for 10 seconds to a maximum of 250 HP

Regeneration- Regenerate 3 HP per second to a max of 250 HP

Hippocratic suggestion- Spawn with 150 HP and 120% speed.

BoneSaw- 100% of knife hits deal 45 extra damage.

KritzKrieg- Gain Kritzkrieg aura for 10 seconds: Medic and all teammates in ward range deal 3x more damage. (ultimate) initial 30 second C/D, then 60 second C/D
