Meet the: Spy

Spawns with Deagle and Knife.


Cloak- Fades into 100% invisibility, lasts 10 seconds, while cloaked Spy is restricted to the knife. (ability) 20 second c/D

Disguise- Spawns with the opposite team's model, has no blue coloring like moles, 100% chance to spawn with ward immunity.

Revolver- 15% chance on hit to deal 3x more damage.

Sapper- 3 wards, wards last whole round. (ability2)

Backstab- Backstabs instantly kill.

Double reverse quadruple agent- Swap teams, change your model to the new enemies model. (ultimate) 25 second initial C/D, then 60 second C/D
