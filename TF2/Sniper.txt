Meet The: Sniper

Spawns with Scout and knife, Scout has 25 round clip only.


Zoom- for every second zoomed in, increase damage by 8.

Reload- Like Force of Nature, gives 7 extra ammo. (ability2) 10 second C/D

Sniper Agility- Spawns with 125 HP and 120% speed.

The Professional- 10% chance on hit to deal 3x more damage.

SMG- Give MP5 for 8 seconds, then give Scout with 25 round clip. (ability) 20 second C/D

SPY!- Become invulnerable to all KNIFE hits for 10 seconds. (ultimate)
