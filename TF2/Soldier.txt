Soldier Durability- Spawn with 200 HP and 95% speed.

Rocket Jump- Relocate from Beholder.

Shock and Awe- 100% chance to deal 200 extra damage with grenades, give Soldier 4 grenades. (ultimate) initial 30 second C/D, then 60 second C/D
