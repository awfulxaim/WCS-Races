Summoner

Ignite
10% chance on hit to set the player on fire for 8 seconds dealing 50% of their race level per second

Exhaust
15% chance on hit to slow the player by 50% and blind them for 6 seconds

Flash
Instanly appear where your crosshair is aiming

Ghost
Increase your speed by 40% for 14 seconds

Revive
50% chance on death to respawn back at base with all of your weapons

Smite
20% chance on hit to deal 20 damage plus 25% of your race level.

Cleanse
When activated purge all debuffs from you and gain invuln for 3 seconds.
