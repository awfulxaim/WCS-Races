Eidolon

A mix of two knife races plus a teleport. :D

Spiritual Essence- Become 100% invisible not including the knife.

Vanish- Teleport, same as level one Relocate from beholder.

Ascension- Flame Predator's on death explosion.

Poltergeist- Disarm from Flame Predator.

Defile- Flame Predator's extra damage.

Shadowstorm- Same as Wisp's Explosion, cannot fail.
