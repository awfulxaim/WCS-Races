Observer

Spawns with and is restricted to USP, has 50 HP.

Light Distortion- 100% Invisibility not including pistol. Same as Beholder's.
Relocate- Far Teleport. Same as Beholder's. (ability)
Command Aura- Added damage, a little stronger than Beholder's.
Anti-Reveal- 100% chance to spawn with Reveal immunity, if Observer is higher level than enemy casting Reveal.
Parachute- 25% gravity.
Produce- Give 8 extra bullets in clip. (ultimate) 15 second C/D
