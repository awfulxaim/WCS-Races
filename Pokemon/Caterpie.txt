Caterpie

Starts with 50 HP

Tackle- Same as Magikarp
String Shot- Decelerator from Timeshifter
Harden- Activated when evolving for the first time. +50 HP, 10 damage reduction from all attacks.
Stun Spore- Can only be activated after evolving twice. Entangling roots. (ability)
PsyBeam- Can only be activated after evolving twice. Fly, give CT autosniper, 24% chance on hit to make drunk and deal 24 dmg. (ability2)
Evolve- Initial 30 second C/D, then 1 minute C/D, on second activation give +100 HP, 24% evasion, and regenerate 1 HP every second. (ultimate)
