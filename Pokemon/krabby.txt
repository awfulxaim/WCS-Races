Krabby

Knife and AK only

Vice Grip
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit with the AK to deal 5-15 additional damage. 5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit with the knife to stun for 2 seconds.

Guillotine
10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit with the knife to deal 10/20/30/40/50 extra damage. If this procs while the enemy is effected by vice grip it instantly kills them.

Bubble Beam
2/4/6/8/10% chance on hit with the AK to slow the enemy by .30 for 5 seconds.

Crabhammer
17/34/51/68/85% chance on hit to deal 10/20/30/40/50% extra damage. With the AK this will only proc if the enemy has been vice gripped in the past 2 seconds.

Harden
Spawn with 20/40/60/80/100 extra HP.

Protect
Reduces the next damage taken by 18/36/54/72/90, also purges debuffs. 10 second CD.

Evolve (ultimate) 1 Level
Grants Krabby an AK. 45 second initial CD.
