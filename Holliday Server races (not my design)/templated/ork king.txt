Ork King

requires total level 720

Ork's Wrath
10/20/30/40% chance on hit to deal 10 to 100% extra damage rolled randomly.

Ork's Feet
Spawn with 40/60/100/200% increased movement speed.

Ork is Hungry
40% chance on hit to leech 22/25/33/50% of the damage you dealt as HP.

Ork Not Die
5/10/25/36% chance on death to respawn where you died 2.5 seconds later. this can proc any number of times.

Ork's Thunder (ultimate)
you deal 32 damage to up to 3/3/4/5 enemies in a 600/750/900/1150 unit radius. 10 second cooldown.
