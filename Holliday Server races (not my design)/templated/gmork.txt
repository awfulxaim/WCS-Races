Gmork

requires total level 610. has a light gray player model.

Gmork's Leap
spawn with long jump. .3/.5/.7/.9/1.1 multiplier.

Black Cloud Camouflage
1/2/3/4/5% chance when hit to become 100% invisible for .5 seconds

Poisonous Fatal Bite
1/2/3/4/5% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 2-7 damage per second for 3 seconds. that damage is rolled randomly.

Adrenaline Rush (ultimate)
Set your speed to 250% for 2/3/4/5/6 seconds. 15 second cooldown.
