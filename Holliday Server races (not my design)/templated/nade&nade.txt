Nade & Nade

Grenades only, no knife. spawns with an he grenade, flashbang, and smoke.

Movement Speed
Spawn with 15/25/35/45/55% increased movement speed.

Wide Explosion
HE grenades deal 25/27/30/32/35 to 45/47/50/52/55 extra damage.

Waves of Fire
20/40/60/80/100% chance to burn enemies hit by your grenade. There is no duration listed. I'm going to assume 2.5 seconds.

Metal Fragments
2/4/6/8/10% chance to blind enemies hit by your grenades for 2 seconds. each enemy hit has their own chance for this to proc on them.

Reload! (utlimate)
You get 1/2/3/4/5 HE grenades, a smoke, and a flashbang. 20/17/15/13/10 second cooldown
