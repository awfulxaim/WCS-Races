Troll Mercenary

requires total level 450

Gravity Shot
12/18/22/33% chance on hit to increase the enemy's gravity by 10/30/40/50% for 3 seconds.

Invisibility Jump
You spawn with long jump. 0/1.1/1.2/1.3x long jump multiplier. while you are in the air you have 61/79/94/98% invisibility.

Multiple Grenades
You spawn with an HE grenade. Whne you throw a HE, you instantly get a new one up to 3/4/5/6 times per spawn.

Grenade Shot (ultimate)
Your next 3/4/5/6 shots spawn an HE grenade explosion where they land. 20 second cooldown.
