Tanuki

requires total level 930. limit of 1 per team.

Shape Shift
11/22/33/44% chance on spawn to set your player model to 1 of 100 different props from the source engine.

Sack of Sake
5/11/22/33% chance on hit to make the enemy drunk (their aim slowly shifts in random directions) and they have 50% increased recoil for 100 seconds.

Cojones
You spawn with 125/150/175/200 HP, Long jump of 1.3/1.5/1.8/2.0, and 8/28/63/74% reduced gravity. while in the air you have 45/75/85/99% invisibility.

Trickster
Create 3 copies of your player model that follow you for 1/2/3/4 seconds. 5 second cooldown.
