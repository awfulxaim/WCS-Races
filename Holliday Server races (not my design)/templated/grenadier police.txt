Grenadier Police

HE grenades only, no knife. Spawns with an HE grenade. gets an HE grenade immediately after throwing one. requires total level 2020.

Take Cover
While crouched you have 51/61/71/81% invisibility.

Grenade Damage
your HE grenades deal 50/60/75/200 extra damage on hit.

Create Fire (ability)
Give yourself a molotov (not an incendiary). 5/4/3/1 second cooldown.

Explosive Barrel (ultimate)
Create an explosive barrel where you are aiming up to 920/920/1000/1080 units away. that barrel lasts 4/4/5/7 seconds. 5/4/3/0 second cooldown.
