King HeadCrab

Invisibility
spawn with 57/61/69/71% invisibility.

Poisonous Bite
18/20/28/33% chance on hit to drug your enemy for .5/1/1.5/2 seconds.

Life Leeching
33% chance on hit to heal for 29/29/30/33% of the damage you dealt.

King Crab (ultimate)
become knife only, set your player model to a poison headcrab from hl2 and set your speed to 1.5/1.7/1.8/2 for 3/5/7/10 seconds. 15 second cooldown.
