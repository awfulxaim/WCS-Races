Athena

requires total level 370

Athena's Spear
10/22/34/40% chance on hit to deal 4/5/6/8 extra damage

Golden Helmet
10/20/30/40% chance to take no damage from non-lethal headshots. this blocks the damage, not evade.

Bright Eyed (ability)
8/13/25/33% chance when you spawn to be able to hold ability to show the outline of enemies through walls.

Athena's Shield
The enemy you are aiming at and all enemies in an 400/500/600/800 unit radius of the enemy you aimed at are blinded and rooted for 2 seconds. 15 second cooldown.
