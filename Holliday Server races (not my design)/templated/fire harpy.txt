Fire Harpy

requires total level 680. has a bright red player model.

Finish Him
2/4/6/8/10% chance on kill to respawn the enemy where they died after a 1 second delay, with HP equal to the last damage you dealt to them. when they respawn they are rooted for 1 second.

Flickering Flames
2/4/6/8/10% chance when hit to become 100% invisible for 1 second.

Fire Blast
2/4/6/8/10% chance on hit to deal 11-16 extra damage rolled randomly.

Curse of the Fire Harpy (ultimate)
You deal 10/15/20/25/30 damage to a random enemy. 25/22/19/17/15 second cooldown.
