Lich King

requires total level 3500.

Defile (ability2)
Lay a 200 unit radius purple ward that lasts 10/15/20/25/30 seconds and deals 10 damage per second. each time an enemy is dealt damage by this ward its radius increases by 50 units. 30 second cooldown.

Harvest Soul
5/10/15/20/25% chance on kill to gain 100 HP.

Necrotic Plague
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 15 seconds. while poisoned they take 2 dmg/sec. if the enemy dies while this is active on them it will jump to the nearest enemy and double its damage. this does not stack.

Raise Dead
20/25/30/35/40% chance on kill to respawn a random teammate.

Ice Lock (ability)
The enemy you are aiming at becomes paralyzed but invulnerable for 2/4/6/8/10 seconds. if the enemy blocks the paralyze effect the invulnerability is lost. 30 second cooldown. enemies affected by this ability have an ice sfx on them.

Raging Spirits
5/10/15/20/25% chance when hit to deal 75% mirror damage.

Infest
5/10/15/20/25% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 7 seconds. this deals 1 damage per second. each second the damage this deals is doubled. if an enemy affected by this skill is healed this effect falls off. this cannot stack

Frostmourne and Shadowmourne (ultimate)
10/20/30/40/50% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 20% for 4 seconds. this stacks with itself. activating this slows all enemies in a 400/450/500/550/600 unit radius by the amount the slowest enemy in that area is slowed by and deals 10 damage to each of them. 30 second cooldown. When you proc a slow on hit it says in the chat "[Frostmourne] you have been slowed by 20% for 4 seconds." When you activate the ultimate it says in chat "[Shadowmourne] You have been slowed by X% for 4 seconds and you took 10 damage."
