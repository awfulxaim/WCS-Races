Dark Templar

Knife only, spawns with 85 HP, cannot buy HP items.

Permanent Cloaking
Spawn with 60/65/70/75/80/85/90/98% invisibility.

Dark Void
1% chance when you spawn to become immune to abilities and ultimates. If this procs, enemies who would affect you with an abiity or ultimate are put to sleep for 10 seconds.

Backstab
10/20/30/40/50/60/70/80% chance when you hit an enemy from behind to deal 75/150/225/300/375/450/525/600 extra damage.

Eradicate
3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10% chance on hit to instantly kill the enemy.
