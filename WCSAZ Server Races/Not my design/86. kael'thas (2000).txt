Kael'thas Sunstrider

Mind Control
1/1/1/2/2/3/4/5% chance on hit to switch the enemies team to yours for the round and give you exp as if you killed them.

Shock Barrier
5/9/13/17/21/25/27/30% chance to evade and deal 10/13/14/17/20/25/25/33% of the damage you would have taken back to the enemy.

Flame Strike
You have a 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance when you hit an enemy to set them on fire, dealing 5 damage every half second for .5/1/1.5/2/2.5/3/3.5/4 seconds.

Nether Beam
3/5/8/10/13/15/17/20% chance on hit to deal 10 extra damage to each enemy in a 200 unit radius of another enemy for each enemy in that radius. NOTE: this does not only deal damage to enemies near the enemy you hit, but to all enemies near another enemy.

Gravity Lapse
You spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% decreased gravity. 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance on hit to set the enemies gravity to 25% for 2 seconds then set it to 200% for 2 seconds.

Summon Phoenix (ultimate)
Respawn the first teammate, in order, who died and is still dead. 55/50/45/40/35/30/25/20 second cooldown. no initial cooldown.
