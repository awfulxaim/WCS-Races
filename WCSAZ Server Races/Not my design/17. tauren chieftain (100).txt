Tauren Chieftain

Shock Wave
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance when hit to deal 10 damage to all enemies in a 200 unit radius. enemies hit by this effect are slowed by 40% for 2 seconds.

War Stomp (ability)
enemies in a 100/150/200/250/300/350 unit radius are rooted for 3 seconds. 30 second cooldown.

Endurance Aura
Spawn with 50/60/70/80/90/100 extra HP.

Reincarnation
You have a 15/25/35/45/55/65/75/80% chance when you die to respawn after 4-6.5 seconds at the location you died at with all of the guns and equipment you had when you died, once per round.
