Maverick Hunter

X-Buster
2/4/6/8/10% chance on hit to deal 2/4/6/8/10 extra damage.

Heart Tank (ability)
Heal for 2/4/6/8/10 HP to a maximum of 240HP. 4 second cooldown.

Force Armor
Gain longjump and add 12/14/16/19/20% chance to deal 5 damage on hit.

Falcon Armor (ability2)
Reduce your gravity by 10/20/30/40/50% and press ability2 to fly indefinitely.

Shadow Armor
Gain 30/40/50/60/70% invisibility and gain 40/50/60/70/80 HP.

Blade Armor
become knife only, increase your movement speed by 10/20/30/40/50% and deal 25 extra damage on hit.

Ultimate Armor
gain longjump and 60/70/80/90/100 HP.

Equip Armor (ultimate)
pick an armor from the menu, you gain its effects. if you pick another armor or the same armor you lose the old effects and gain or reapply the new ones. 30 second initial cooldown. 30 second cooldown. 1 level.
