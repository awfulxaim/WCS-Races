Shadow Priest

Devouring Plague
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 5 damage every second for 5 seconds. you heal for poison damage they take.

Mind Flay
3/5/8/10/13/15/18/20% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 5 damage every second for 10 seconds.

Flash Heal (ability)
heal yourself for 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40 HP. 10 second cooldown.

Psychic Horror
1/2/3/4/5/6/7/8% chance on hit to disarm the enemy and slow them by 60% for 6 seconds.

Inner Fire
Spawn with 8/12/16/20/24/28/32/36% increased movement speed.

Shadowform (ultimate)
You cannot use your ability for the rest of the round but you deal 15 extra damage on hit, your poison's damage is doubled, and psychic horror roots for 2 seconds then slows them by 80% for 4 seconds. 60 second initial cooldown. can only be used once per round.
