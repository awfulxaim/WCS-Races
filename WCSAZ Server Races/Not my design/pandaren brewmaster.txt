Pandaren Brewmaster

Breath of Fire
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 3 seconds. 5dmg/0.5s

Drunken Haze
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50/60/70/85 extra HP.

Drunken Brawler
5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance on hit to deal 5 extra damage.

Storm
30% chance on hit to deal 30/35/40/45/50/55/60/65% extra damage.

Earth and Fire (ultimate)
for 10 seconds you gain 100 HP, 100% chance to proc breath of fire and drunken brawler, and get .05/.1/.15/.2/.25/.3/.35/.4 increased movement speed. if you would have less than 1 HP after 10 seconds, you die. 40 second cooldown.
