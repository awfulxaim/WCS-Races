Hunter

scout only, no knife.

Hunter's Mark
When you spawn a random enemy is chosen and a chat notification tells you their name. they take 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% extra damage this round. that player gets a message that they have been marked and take extra damage this round.

Flare (ability)
lay a 250/300/350/400/450/500/550/600 unit radius ward where you are aiming. that ward sets enemies to 100% visibility for 10 seconds if they enter it and the ward lasts 5 seconds. 20 second cooldown.

Serpent Sting
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 5 damage per second for 7 seconds.

Lightning Reflexes
4/8/12/16/20/24/28/33% chance to evade.

Multi-shot
15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50% chance on hit to deal the damage you dealt to that enemy to all other enemies in a 400 unit radius of the enemy you hit.

Trueshot Aura
30% chance on hit to deal 10/15/20/20/30/40/50/60% more damage.
