Snorlax

Snore
You have a 100% chance when hit while asleep to deal 15/17/19/21/23/25 damage to the enemy who attacked you.

Body Slam
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to deal 30/35/40/50/60/70% extra damage and bash for 1.5 seconds.

Yawn
You heal 5/6/7/8/9/10 HP per second while you are asleep. You can aim while asleep.

Thick Fat
Spawn with 150/160/170/180/190/200 HP. this is your max HP.

Rest (ultimate)
fall asleep for 8 seconds. You instantly heal for 20 HP. While you are asleep you cannot shoot, aim, (unless you have Yawn) or move but take no damage. your screen is darkened by 50% while asleep. 25/23/21/19/17/15 second cooldown.
