Paladin

Holy Light (ability)
Heal the teammate you are aiming at for 3/6/9/12/15 HP. if you are not aiming at a teammate you are healed instead. 15 second cooldown.

Devotion Aura
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50 more HP.

Hand of Freedom
Spawn with 15/20/25/30/35% more speed.

Divine Shield
Become invulnerable for 1/2/3/4/5 seconds. 30 second cooldown.
