Goblin Alchemist

spawns with a grenade

Healing Spray (ability)
Heal the teammate you are aiming at for 15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50% of their current HP. if you are not aiming at anyone this heals you instead to a max of 150HP. 10 second cooldown.

Chemical Rage
spawn with 4/8/12/16/20/24/28/32% increased movement speed.

Acid Bomb
2/4/6/8/10/12/14/15% chance on hit to poison the enemy for 10 seconds, dealing 5 damage per second. 100% chance when an enemy takes damage from an exploding barrel or grenade to be poisoned.

Transmute (ultimate)
Spawn an explosive barrel. 15/14/13/12/11/10/9/8 second cooldown.
