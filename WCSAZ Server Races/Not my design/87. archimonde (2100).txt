Archimonde

Airburst
6/8/10/12/14/16/18/20% chance on hit to impale the enemy really high into the air. enough that they take 5-10 fall damage when they land.

Soul Charge
You spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% increased movement speed.

Grip of the Legion
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/24% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 25/30/35/40/45/50/55/60% for 4 seconds. after the 4 seconds the enemy gets their movement speed back 10% at a time every second.

Fear (ability)
All enemies on the map are pushed away from you hard once every second for 6 seconds. 31/30/29/28/27/26/25/24 second cooldown.

Finger of Death
5% chance on hit to deal 25/50/75/100/125/150/175/200 extra damage.

Doomfire (ultimate)
for the next 6 seconds, any enemy that comes within 400 units of you takes 4/8/12/16/20/24/28/30 damage per second.
