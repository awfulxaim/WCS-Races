Algalon the Observer

Phase Punch
20% chance on hit to impale the enemy and deal 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% of the enemies current HP in damage.

Cosmic Smash (ability)
Create a 150 unit radius ward centered on a random enemies current position. this ward deals 10 damage per second and lasts 8 seconds. 32/30/28/26/24/22/20/18 second cooldown.

Collapsing Star
HE grenades turn into black holes after they explode. black holes are 285 unit wards that pull all enemies on the map toward it once every second. the black holes last 3 seconds for 4 total pulses. .25 seconds after each pulse there is another grenade-like explosion which deals damage exactly like a grenade.

Quantum Strike
6/8/10/12/14/16/18/20% chance on hit to deal 50% extra damage and increase your speed by .05 for the rest of the round.

Celestial Power (ability2)
Deal 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25 damage to all enemies. 30 second cooldown.

Big Bang (ultimate)
Root yourself and become unable to shoot for 15 seconds. After 15 seconds you deal 6000 damage to all enemies in a 300/400/500/600/700/800/900/1000 unit radius. There is an audio warning which plays when you activate this and makes a noise coming from you every 3 seconds. once per round cooldown. 35 second initial cooldown.
