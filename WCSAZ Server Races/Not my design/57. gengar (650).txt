Gengar

Lick
4/8/12/16/20/24/28/32% chance on hit to blind the enemy with a 75% opacity blue screen for 3 seconds.

Hypnosis
3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10% chance on hit to put the enemy to sleep for 3 seconds. preventing them from moving, turning, shooting or taking damage. their screen is darkened exactly like Lick when they are asleep and their player model is turned dark blue.

Confuse Ray
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/24% chance on hit to banish the target for 2.5s.

Night Shade
3/5/8/10/13/15/17/20% chance on hit to deal extra damage to the enemy equal to Gengar's level.

Levitate
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% reduced gravity.

Dream Eater (ultimate)
Deal 10/12/14/16/18/20/22/25 damage to all sleeping enemies. You heal for 50% of the total damage dealt. 28/26/24/22/20/18/16/14 second cooldown.
