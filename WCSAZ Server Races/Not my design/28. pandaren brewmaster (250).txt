Pandaren Brewmaster

Breath of Fire
3/6/9/12/15/18/21/25% chance on hit to burn the enemy for 3 seconds. 5dmg/0.5s

Drunken Haze
Spawn with 10/20/30/40/50/60/70/85 extra HP, you are drunk. (being drunk means that your screen slowly rotates left and right, and your aim moves in a sideways figure-8 pattern)

Drunken Brawler
5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance on hit to deal 5 extra damage.

Storm
30% chance on hit to deal 30/35/40/45/50/55/60/65% extra damage.

Earth and Fire (ultimate)
Heal 100 HP to a max of 185, then for the next 10 seconds you have: 100% chance to proc breath of fire and drunken brawler, and get .05/.1/.15/.2/.25/.3/.35/.4 increased movement speed. 40 second cooldown.
