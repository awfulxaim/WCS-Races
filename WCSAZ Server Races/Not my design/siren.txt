Siren

Cure (ability)
Heal the teammate you are aiming at for 3/6/9/12/15 HP. if no teammate is aimed at you heal yourself. 15 second cooldown.

Sleep Song
2/4/6/8/10% chance on hit to put the enemy to sleep for 3 seconds. Slept enemies are blinded with a blue screen, their player model turns blue and they play a sleeping sound effect. slept enemies are invulnerable for the duration but cannot move or shoot.

Protect
5/10/15/20/25% chance to evade.

Harmony
You and teammates in a 250 unit radius are healed for 5HP every 2 seconds. This only activates if there is a teammate in the area.

Silent Voice
80/85/90/95/100% chance when you spawn to give your entire team ultimate immunity.
