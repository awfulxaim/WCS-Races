Spirit Harvest
You have a 60/70/80/90/100% chance on kill to gain a soul. If that enemy has killed someone this round you gain one additional soul. You do not gain souls from killing bots.

Soul Channel
When you spawn gain 2/4/6/8/10 HP for each soul you have, to no maxmimum.

Rebirth
When you die, if you have at least 8/7/6/5/4 souls, lose that many souls and respawn at your spawn with the weapons and equipment you died with.

Phantom Soul
You spawn with .01/.02/.03/.04/.05 increased movement speed for each soul you have to a max of 1.75 and you spawn with 1/2/3/4/5% increased invisibility for each soul you have to a max of 75%.

Soul Eater (ultimate)
trade 8/7/6/5/4 souls to gain each of these benefits for 10 seconds:
double the chance of death scythe to proc and it deals 10 extra damage instead.
increase your movement speed and invisibility by 10%.
reduce your gravity by 25%.
increase your HP by 2/4/6/8/10 for each soul you have.
long jump.
