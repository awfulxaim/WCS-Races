Neptulon

Frost Storm
20% chance on hit to slow the enemy and all enemies in a 200/300/350/400/450/500/550/600 unit radius of that enemy by 20/30/35/40/45/50/55/60% for 6 seconds.

Chilling Touch
3/6/9/12/15/28/25% chance on hit to slow the enemy by 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% for 4 seconds. if the enemy is already affected by frost storm, set their movement speed to 0 for 4 seconds instead.

Ice Block
5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance when hit to evade. when you evade you cannot move or shoot for 1 second but you are invulnerable. you are still effected by gravity while you cannot move or shoot. you have an ice vfx animation covering you while you are invulnerable.

Tidal Wave
Spawn with 4/8/12/16/20/24/28/32% increased movement speed.

Water Shield
Spawn with 30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100 more HP.

Vortex
20/30/40/50/60/70/80/90% chance when you die to lay a 150 unit radius blue ward. If an enemy steps in the radius the ward is destroyed and a black hole is spawned in that location. the black hole is the same as the black hole from Dark Messenger on Diablos but smaller.
