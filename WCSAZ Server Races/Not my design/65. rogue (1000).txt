Rogue

knife only

Stealth (ability)
you spawn with 100% invisibility until you are shot or you attack. activating this ability re-applies this. 23/22/21/20/19/18/17/16 second cooldown.

Camouflage
30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100% chance to spawn with the enemy team's player model.

Hemorrhage
5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40% chance on hit to bleed the enemy for 6 seconds. enemies that are bleeding take 5 damage per second for the duration and are slowed by 60% of whatever their movement speed is for 1.5 seconds every time they take damage. (example: if their movement speed is 1.2 they would be slowed to .72 every time they take damage) there is a bleeding animation and a hurt sound each time a bleeding enemy takes damage.

Kidney Shot
4/8/12/16/20/24/28/32% chance on hit to bash the enemy for 2 seconds.

Backstab
30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100% chance when you stab an enemy in the back to deal 600 extra damage to them.

Sprint (ability2)
gain .1/.2/.3/.4/.5/.6/.7/.75% increased movement speed for 2 seconds. 10 second cooldown.

Lightning Reflexes
4/8/12/16/20/24/28/33% chance to evade. when you evade you do not lose stealth.

Shadowstep (ultimate)
Instantly teleport behind the player (enemy or teammate) you are aiming at. 25/24/24/23/22/21/19/18 second cooldown. Killing an enemy you have Shadowstepped to resets this cooldown.
