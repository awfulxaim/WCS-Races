Shaman

Chain Heal
Heal your entire team for 1/1/1/1/2/2/2/3 HP per second to a max of 100 HP.

Earth Fire Wind Air (ability)
Lay four 200 radius wards in a square 100 units around you. each ward deals 15 damage per second and last 10 seconds. once per round.

Nature's Guardian
Spawn with 30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100 more HP and 30/40/50/60/70/80/90/100 armor and helmet.

Reincarnation
You have a 15/25/35/45/55/65/75/80% chance when you die to respawn after 4-6.5 seconds (rolled randomly) at the location you died at with all of the guns and equipment you had when you died, once per round.

Earth Shield
4/8/12/16/20/25% chance to evade.

Bloodlust (ultimate)
You entire team gets 3/4/5/6/7/8/9/10 extra damage and 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/24% increased movement speed for 10 seconds. 45 second cooldown.
