Highlord Tirion Fordring

Supreme Commander
Spawn with 4/10/15/20/25/30/35/40 extra HP. 100% chance on hit to deal 2/4/6/8/10/12/16/20% more damage.

Purify (ability)
Heal targeted player for 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40 HP, if no one is targeted this heals yourself. players healed by purify have all debuffs removed from them. 15 second cooldown.

Holy Ground (ability2)
lay a 200 unit radius ward, this ward deal no damage to enemies, instead it heals you and your teammates who enter its radius for 2/4/6/8/10/12/14/16 HP per second, the radius increases by 25 for each person it heals each second. If an enemy enters the ward area they cannot use abilities and ultimates until they leave the ward area. lasts 15 seconds. 30 second cooldown.

Ashbringer
1/1/1/2/2/3/4/5% chance to deal 750% damage, has the vaporization effect of the gnomish death ray.

The Light
You and teammates in a 300 unit radius gain 1/1/1/2/2/3/4/5 HP per second.

Final Stand (ultimate)
Become invulnerable for 4 seconds, give +5 damage to all attacks during this time. 85/80/75/65/60/55/50/45 Second cooldown.
