Bloodseeker

Rupture (ability)
Every enemy bleeds for 5 damage per second and is slowed by 60% until they stop moving. 45/40/35/30/25/20 second cooldown.

Blood Bath
Gain 10/15/20/25/30/35 HP when you kill an enemy.

Blood Thirst
Gain .05/.1/.15/.2/.25./.3 movement speed for 12 seconds when you kill an enemy. this stacks.

Vampiric Breath
33% chance on hit to heal for 10/20/30/40/50/60% of the damage you deal.

Lethal Rend
5/10/15/20/25/30% chance on hit to bleed the enemy for 6 seconds. enemies that are bleeding take 5 damage per second for the duration and are slowed by 60% of whatever their movement speed is for 1.5 seconds every time they take damage. (example: if their movement speed is 1.2 they would be slowed to .72 every time they take damage) there is a bleeding animation and a hurt sound each time a bleeding enemy takes damage.

Bloodrage
Gain 10/15/20/25/30/35 HP. Increase your movement speed by .05/.1/.15/.2/.25./.3 for 12 seconds. 40 second cooldown.
