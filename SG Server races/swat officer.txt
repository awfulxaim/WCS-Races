S.W.A.T. Officer

requires total level 250. limited to 2 per team. cannot buy flaming gloves of warmth, long jump, boots of speed, or ring of armor.

Dexterity
Spawn with 5/10/15/20/25% increased movement speed.

Combat Training
Spawn with 100/125/150/175/200 armor and helmet and 5/10/15/20/25% damage reduction from all sources.

Armory
20/40/60/80/100% chance on spawn to get an M4A1-S.

Strength
100% chance on hit to deal 10/14/18/21/25% more damage. this is not considered extra damage for the purposes of blocking extra damage and calculating weapon damage. (that means that leech considers this damage when it leeches)

Stun Grenade
Your HE and Flashbangs slow enemies damaged or blinded by it by 20/30/40/50/60% for 3 seconds.

Rallying Cry (ultimate)
For the next 6/7/8/9/10 seconds you gain 5/10/15/20/25% additional increased movement speed and a 5/10/15/20/25% chance to evade. 30 second cooldown.
