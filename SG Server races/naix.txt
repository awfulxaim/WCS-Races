Naix

requires total level 350. limited to 2 per team. cannot by long jump, mask of death, or HP.

Infest
You gain 5/10/15/20 HP when you kill an enemy. You instantly teleport to the location they were at when you killed them if you were crouched when you killed them.

Blood Bath
Add 10/20/30/40 HP to the HP gained from your other skills.

Feast
You heal for 4/6/8/10% of the enemy's HP on hit.

Rage (ultimate)
Your fire rate is increased by 15/25/35/40% for 3/4/5/6 seconds. 20 second cooldown.
