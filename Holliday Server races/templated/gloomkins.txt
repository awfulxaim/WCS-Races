Gloomkins

requires total level 2010.

Save Weapons
You always spawn with the weapons you had when you spawned last round. This doesn't activate on the first round you spawn as Gloomkins or the first round after changing races to Gloomkins.

Speed
Spawn with 50/80/100/150% increased movement speed

Invisibility
Spawn with 21/61/71/78% invisibility.

Teleport
Become 100% invisible and lose your weapons. Ignore gravity and push yourself constantly in the direction you are aiming. the max distance you can travel during this is 820/920/1000/1080 units or 3/4/5/7 second. press ultimate again to cancel all of these effects. no cooldown.
