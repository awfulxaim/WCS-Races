Lord Voldemort

requires total level 870. P2000 only.

Avada Kedavra
5/10/15/20% chance on hit to deal 200 extra damage. this has a green beam effect and causes the enemy to glow green.

Crucio
10/15/20/25% chance on hit to deal 4/9/14/19 damage per second to the enemy for 4 seconds.

Reducto
5/10/15/18% chance on spawn for your shots to cause HE grenade explosions where they hit for the round

Teleport (ultimate)
instantly teleport to a location you are aiming at up to 750/920/1000/2600 units away. no cooldown.
