Broodmother

requires total level 400. cannot buy amulet of the cat

Spawn Spiderlings
18/22/28/33% chance on hit to deal 5/8/12/15 extra damage.

Spin Web
Spawn with 20/30/40/50% increased movement speed and 22/41/61/71% invisibility.

Incapacitating Bite
18/22/28/33% chance on hit to deal 5/8/12/15 extra damage and draw a cylinder of beams around the player making them unable to see if they dont move out of the tube.

Insatiable Hunger (ultimate)
Set your speed to 180% for 5/7/10/12 seconds. for that duration you have a 100% chance on hit to heal for 5-15 HP rolled randomly. once per round.
